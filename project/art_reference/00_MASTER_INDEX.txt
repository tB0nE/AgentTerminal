STYLE GUIDE SUMMARY
================================================================================

VISUAL STYLE:
  - African cyberpunk aesthetic
  - Township realism with futuristic elements
  - Contrast: poverty vs. corporate wealth
  - Colors: Earth tones + neon accents
  - Mood: Gritty but hopeful

KEY VISUAL CONTRASTS:
  - Township warmth vs. Corporate coldness
  - Makeshift construction vs. Pristine technology
  - Community life vs. Isolation
  - Dust and decay vs. Digital transcendence

AUDIO STYLE:
  - Electronic ambient with African influences
  - Township soundscape authenticity
  - Digital glitches for implant effects
  - Emotional resonance in music
