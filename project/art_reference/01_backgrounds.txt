================================================================================
ART REFERENCE: BACKGROUNDS - DUNOON 2056
================================================================================

This document contains all required background images for the game.
Each background should be 1920x1080 pixels minimum, PNG format with possible
parallax layers where noted.

================================================================================
MENU & TRANSITION SCREENS
================================================================================

1. TITLE_SCREEN
   - Location: Game opening title screen
   - Mood: Cyberpunk noir, dystopian atmosphere
   - Lighting: Dark with neon accents, low ambient light
   - Details: 
     * View of Dunoon township at night from elevated position
     * Distant Cape Town skyscrapers glowing on horizon
     * Neon signs in foreground with broken/flickering effect
     * Power lines cutting across frame
     * Stars partly obscured by smog/pollution
   - Text overlay area: Center-bottom for game title "DUNOON 2056"

2. GENERIC_BLACK
   - Location: Transition screen, game over
   - Mood: Void, emptiness, digital darkness
   - Lighting: Pure black with subtle digital noise/static
   - Details:
     * Near-total blackness
     * Optional: Very faint grid pattern suggesting digital void
     * Occasional glitch artifacts at edges
   - Text overlay area: Full screen for text display

3. TRANSITION
   - Location: Scene transition overlay
   - Mood: Glitchy, digital interference
   - Details:
     * Static noise pattern
     * Scan lines
     * Corrupted data visualization
   - Format: Overlay image for transition.png

4. CREDITS_SCREEN
   - Location: End game credits
   - Mood: Melancholic, reflective
   - Lighting: Dark with soft ambient glow
   - Details:
     * Abstract digital patterns
     * Faint silhouette of Dunoon skyline
     * Stars or data streams in background

================================================================================
DUNOON TOWNSHIP - EXTERIOR LOCATIONS
================================================================================

5. SHACK_EXTERIOR
   - Location: Player's home shack exterior view
   - Time: Morning (can have variants for afternoon/evening)
   - Mood: Gritty realism, poverty, but with signs of life
   - Lighting: Warm morning sunlight filtering through dust
   - Weather: Clear with dust particles in air
   - Details:
     * Small corrugated iron shack (3m x 4m)
     * Makeshift construction: wood scraps, plastic sheeting
     * Cracked window with dirty glass
     * Hand-painted sign nearby: "MAMA NOMI'S PLACE"
     * Power cables running along posts
     * Dusty ground with scattered debris
     * Other shacks visible in background
     * Distant skyscrapers on horizon
   - Parallax layers: Foreground debris, mid shack, background township

6. SHACK_INTERIOR
   - Location: Player's home interior
   - Time: Variable (morning/afternoon/evening/night variants needed)
   - Mood: Cramped, impoverished, but lived-in and personal
   - Lighting: 
     * Morning: Thin beams through cracks in walls
     * Afternoon: Harsh, stifling heat visible in air
     * Evening: Golden light, long shadows
     * Night: Dark, lit only by faint implant glow
   - Details:
     * Corrugated iron ceiling, rust-stained
     * Walls of iron, wood scraps, plastic sheeting
     * Thin mattress on floor
     * Camp stove in corner
     * Bucket of water
     * Canned food on shelf
     * Battered neural deck under tarp
     * Faded photograph of woman on wall
     * Single cracked, dirty window
     * View of maze of shacks through window
     * Hidden compartment under floorboard
   - Key interaction points: Photo, mattress, floorboard, deck location

7. GAME_MAP_SCREEN
   - Location: Overview map of Dunoon township
   - Time: Variable based on game time
   - Mood: Functional, navigational, atmospheric
   - Lighting: Top-down view with ambient township lighting
   - Details:
     * Isometric or stylized top-down view of township
     * Key locations marked:
       - Home Shack
       - Mama Nomi's Place
       - Tango's Repair Shop
       - Net Cafe
       - Market
       - Clinic
       - Abandoned Lot
       - Wine Farm (distant)
       - Beach (edge of map)
     * Roads and pathways between locations
     * Distant Cape Town skyline on one edge
     * Ocean on another edge
   - Interactive: Button locations for each destination
