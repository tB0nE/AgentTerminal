VOICE AUDIO NOTES
================================================================================

The game uses text-based dialogue with internal voices. If voice acting is added:

VOICE DIRECTION - THE HACKER
----------------------------
Tone: Analytical, curious, sometimes cold
Pace: Quick, precise
Quality: Slightly processed/digital edge
Emotional range: Controlled, intellectual excitement

VOICE DIRECTION - THE SURVIVOR
------------------------------
Tone: Wary, practical, sometimes paranoid
Pace: Guarded, cautious
Quality: Grittier, street-hardened
Emotional range: Suspicious to protective

VOICE DIRECTION - THE ANCESTOR
------------------------------
Tone: Patient, wise, poetic
Pace: Slow, measured
Quality: Warm, resonant, slightly reverbed
Emotional range: Compassionate to stern

VOICE DIRECTION - NPCS
----------------------
General direction: South African accents appropriate to characters
- Mama Nomi: Warm, matronly, Xhosa-influenced
- Tango: Technical, younger, urban
- Silas: Hardened, guarded, resistance fighter
- Street kids: Quick, youthful, street-wise

================================================================================
AUDIO IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
================================================================================

LAYERING SYSTEM:
- Layer 1: Ambient sounds (continuous)
- Layer 2: Music (situational)
- Layer 3: Sound effects (event-triggered)

TRANSITION RULES:
- Music should fade between tracks (2 second crossfade)
- Ambient sounds should persist across location changes when appropriate
- Sound effects should always play immediately

VOLUME LEVELS (relative):
- Music: 60-80%
- Ambient: 40-60%
- Sound Effects: 100%
- Voice (if added): 100%

IMPLANT EFFECTS:
- When implant is glitching, add subtle distortion to all audio
- Critical implant state: periodic warning sound layered
- Implant death: audio fade to static then silence

================================================================================
TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Format: OGG Vorbis or MP3
Quality: 192kbps minimum for music, 128kbps for effects
Sample Rate: 44.1kHz
Channels: Stereo for music, mono acceptable for effects

Loop Points: All ambient and music tracks should have clean loop points
File Naming: lowercase_with_underscores
