NEURAL DECK
-----------
Description: Essential hacking tool, player's primary equipment
First Appearance: Found in shack or purchased from Tango

Visual Design:
  - Form factor: Tablet-sized device, battered but functional
  - Age: Old model, well-used
  - Dimensions: Approximately 25cm x 15cm x 3cm
  - Materials: Metal casing with exposed circuitry
  - Color scheme: Gunmetal gray, copper accents, glowing indicator lights
  - Details:
    * Screen (cracked but working)
    * Cable port for neural connection
    * Antenna or signal extension
    * Wear marks showing heavy use
    * Custom modifications (hacker's personal touches)
    * Stickers or scratches from previous owner

States:
  1. Idle (screen off, indicator light)
  2. Active (screen glowing, data visible)
  3. Connected (cable attached, active hacking)
  4. Damaged (optional variant)

View: Icon for inventory, possibly larger view when examining
